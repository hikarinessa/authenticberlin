Our Practices

Discover transformative modalities that foster deeper connection, presence, and authenticity.

Authentic Relating

Authentic Relating is a set of practices designed to build awareness of yourself in relation to others, fostering a greater capacity for connection. These practices help you:

  • Communicate with honesty and openness
  • Stay connected during challenging interactions
  • Recognize and express your true feelings and needs
  • Listen deeply to others without judgment

Through structured exercises, you'll experience what it means to be fully present with others in ways that transcend ordinary social interactions.

Circling

Circling is a dynamic, present-moment awareness practice where a small group of people focus their attention on what's happening in the moment – both internally and in relation to others. It's sometimes described as "meditation with other people."

In a Circle, you might:

  • Explore your direct experience as it unfolds
  • Practice deep listening and authentic expression
  • Develop your capacity to stay present with discomfort
  • Experience being fully seen and acknowledged

About Luz

I'm an experienced video games developer with a passion for relational and mindfulness practices.

I've always been drawn to games in all their forms: board games, social games, role-playing games, etc. A context with clear structure and rules has the power to ground me, awakens my curiosity, minimizes my social anxiety, and allows for my creativity and personal expression to flourish.

When I discovered Authentic Relating games an incredible gate opened for me. I could harness that same openness and apply it to the way I create and cultivate friendships —in a more conscious, intentional and genuine way. The practice of Circling® propelled me forward once more, uncovering a wealth of inner wisdom and personal growth.

After these transformative experiences I devoted myself to helping others create and nurture healthier relationships. I ran more than 18 workshops over the course of the following year, and I became a certified Circling facilitator by Circling Europe.

My aim is to create spaces where individuals can authentically connect, grow, and learn with one another.
